Robotics

I am fascinated by the integration of the virtual and the tangible -- the future of engineering. I am heavily involved in Midwestern Regional Design Competition hosted under iRobotics here at UIUC. We design and build a robot in scratch to compete against other universities. Robotics has the potential to revoluntionize safety and the day-to-day life of third world countries, creating a paradigm shift unlike any other in the 21st century.

Computer Science

Computer Science is integral to every aspect of modern engineering, whether its sequencing genomes or predicting the stock market. I plan on taking AI and ML courses to further my knowledge in this career area.

My Projects

FaceTunesmore_vert

This app analyzes emotion from a picture and plays a corresponding Spotify playlist.

FaceTunesclose

Microsoft's Emotion API returns the strength of every emotion present in an image. The application parses this data and selects the strongest emotion and links it with a Spotify playlist based on the strongest emotion. This playlist is then played and it can be favorited or blocked from future queries.

Wireless Light Switchmore_vert

An installation-free solution to control any light switch wirelessly.

Wireless Light Switchclose

This device integrates an Arduino and Bluetooth with an innovative 3D-printed design to allow for wireless control of any light switch from the outside. The device sticks onto the switch plate and physically flips the switch, and it runs on AAA batteries, so the installation does not require any electical expertise.
